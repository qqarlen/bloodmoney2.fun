About BLOODMONEY 2 (Human Expenditure Program)

BLOODMONEY 2, also known as Human Expenditure Program, is a psychological horror simulation mixing suspense, drama, and ethical dilemmas. Released on Sep 12, 2025 by SHROOMYCHRIST on itch, the game follows Harvey Harvington through cute visuals and dark undertones. Every choice tests morality, responsibility, and empathy, while minigames and Eun‑Mi’s presence shape the story. Explore this twisted virtual pet experience and uncover hidden truths—play now and face the consequences of your decisions.

Unlock the Core Story

Harvey Harvington exists as a lost program under his wife, Eun‑Mi Harvington, guiding the Human Expenditure Program. Uncover his tragic past through interactions and mini‑games. Your choices create suspense and moral tension, confronting themes of guilt and responsibility.

  • Two possible endings shaped by your ethics
  • Five minigames that earn money and reveal fragments
  • Feeding and sleep management affecting mood and outcomes

Gameplay: Be Eun‑Mi to take care of Harvey

  • Play five unique minigames to influence Harvey’s mood and the narrative.
  • Feed Harvey with candy, bread, donut, pie, cake, or a big salad to affect well‑being.
  • Manage sleep for memory reconstruction and readiness.
  • Simple controls: play entirely with your mouse button.
